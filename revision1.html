Description I was born in China in October! I had my first day of kindergarten. I was nervous to go but it turned out to be the start of an ongoing 13-year apocalypse! I don't remember it too well but I know it was a relaxed day. I hoped it stayed that way throughout my schooling, but it didnt't. I took a road trip from Jackson all the way to San Diego, CA. I went through Arkansas, Oklahoma, Texas, New Mexico, and Arizona. I spend about a week there and then had to ride all the way back home. I saw the Grand Canyon and Petrified Forest National Parks as well as some zoos, museums, and Meteor Crater! I flew from New York City to Barcelona, Spain. I stayed there for a few days and saw La Sagrada Familia. I then went aboard a cruise ship towards France and Italy. I saw Pompeii, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and Rome! While I was in Rome, I visited the Vatican! I also visited Nice in France and had a tour there. I went to Governor's School for Computational Physics this summer where a learned (and forgot) a lot of new things! Although very basic, I learned how to code in Fortran. I also met a lot of interesting people from around Tennessee. It was a very hard class and I barely understood most of the things taught. I wouldn't have made it through without the amazing mentors that were always there to help us. It was a very memorable summer!
